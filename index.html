See a new World Through the Apple Vision Pro headset

Welcome to the world of new and innovative reality through the lens of the Apple Vision Pro Headset. The Apple Vision Pro headset is a new mixed-reality headset coming out of the technology company Apple. Announced on June 5, 2023 and finally launched on February 2, 2024. The device runs visionOS, a mixed-reality operating system derived from iOS frameworks using a 3D user interface; it supports multitasking via windows that appear to float within the user's surroundings,as seen by cameras built into the headset.
